CASE STUDY 1: Innovate Corp - Enterprise Software Transformation
================================================================
Customer: Innovate Corp (CUST_0087)
Industry: Transportation
Company Size: Enterprise (1000+)
Deal Value: $646,313 (DEAL_0082)
Products: Enterprise Suite (Software Licenses)
Deal Owner: Sales Rep 2

Challenge:
Innovate Corp, a major transportation and logistics company, was managing operations across 35 distribution centers using a patchwork of spreadsheets, legacy scheduling software, and manual processes. Route optimization took hours, shipment tracking was unreliable, and managers had no real-time visibility into fleet performance.

Solution:
We deployed Enterprise Suite across all 35 locations with custom modules for fleet management, route planning, and shipment tracking. The rollout included a phased migration over 12 weeks, starting with the headquarters and expanding region by region. Integration with their existing SAP ERP was completed using our API gateway.

Results:
- 40% reduction in route planning time through automated optimization
- Real-time visibility into 2,000+ active shipments
- $1.2M annual savings from consolidated software licenses (replaced 6 point solutions)
- 98% user adoption within 90 days of go-live

Key Quote: "Enterprise Suite gave us a single source of truth across all our distribution centers. We went from making decisions on day-old data to real-time insights." - VP of Operations, Innovate Corp

Sales Insights: The deal started as a $200K proposal for a single hub. After a successful 60-day pilot at their Chicago facility, the customer expanded to an enterprise-wide deployment. The pilot-first approach was critical to winning executive buy-in.
