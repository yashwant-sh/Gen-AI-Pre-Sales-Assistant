SOFTWARE LICENSES
=================

HARDWARE
========

Network Switch
--------------
Enterprise-grade managed switches for data center and campus networking, supporting 10G/25G/100G Ethernet with advanced routing and security features.

Key Features:
- Layer 2/3 switching with BGP, OSPF, and VXLAN support
- 10G/25G/100G port configurations
- Zero-touch provisioning with centralized management
- MACsec encryption for in-flight data protection
- Energy-efficient design with smart power management
- Stacking support for up to 8 units

Pricing: From $4,500 to $43,000 per unit depending on port count and speed configuration.

Ideal For: Organizations building or upgrading data center and campus networks. Popular in Technology, Finance (trading floor networks), and Education (campus-wide deployments).


Storage Array
-------------
High-performance unified storage arrays supporting block, file, and object protocols with inline deduplication and compression.

Key Features:
- All-flash and hybrid configurations
- Inline deduplication and compression (typical 4:1 data reduction)
- Synchronous and asynchronous replication
- NVMe-oF support for sub-100 microsecond latency
- Non-disruptive upgrades and expansion
- Integration with VMware, Hyper-V, and container platforms

Pricing: From $29,000 to $47,000 depending on capacity, performance tier, and data services.

Ideal For: Organizations requiring high-performance shared storage. Healthcare (PACS imaging), Manufacturing (CAD/CAM), and Finance (transaction databases) are primary verticals.


Server Rack
-----------
Pre-configured and custom rack solutions including compute nodes, power distribution, cooling, and cable management for data center deployments.

Key Features:
- 42U standard and 48U high-density configurations
- Integrated power distribution with dual-feed redundancy
- Hot/cold aisle containment compatible
- Cable management with color-coded patching
- Remote monitoring with environmental sensors (temperature, humidity, power)
- Tool-less rail kits for rapid deployment

Pricing: Starting at approximately $22,600 per rack depending on configuration and compute density.

Ideal For: Organizations building or expanding data center capacity. Particularly popular with Technology companies, Government agencies, and large Enterprise customers across all industries.


Workstation

High-performance desktop and mobile workstations for engineering, design, data science, and content creation professionals.

Key Features:
